Resumé Research Courses


My work focuses on designing practically efficient cryptographic protocols that address the dual concerns of privacy and accountability, with a particular emphasis on advanced authentication primitves, threshold cryptosystems and post-quantum cryptography.

I am also interested in computational number theory, especially in ways that concern the arithmetic of elliptic curves, and its applications to cryptography.


I am looking for students and postdocs interested in working on research projects in the areas of security and privacy, broadly construed. Please reach out if this is something that interests you!
