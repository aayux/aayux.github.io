Resumé

I completed my PhD at George Mason University where I was fortunate to be advised by Foteini Baldimtsi. I also spent three wonderful years as a visiting fellow at the University of Wisconsin, Madison hosted by Rishab Goyal. During my PhD, I worked with the research teams at Mysten Labs, Category Labs and the Input Output Group. I received my Master's degree in Computer Science from Rutgers University, Camden and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Pune.
