Courses: Approximation Algorithms


Home     Courses

Syllabus with some notes, slides and exercise problems on Approximation Algorithms based largely on Vijay Vazirani's textbook. Please reach out to me via email for any comments, questions and/or correction to the material.

Course Objective: To attain sufficient understanding of underlying combinatorial structure of various algorithmic problems, to be able to fully analyse said problems more generally and in the context of approximation.

Textbook(s):

  1. Approximation Algorithms
    Vijay V. Vazirani (2003)

  2. Combinatorial Optimization: Algorithms and Complexity
    Christos H. Papadimitriou, Kenneth Steiglitz (1982, 1998)
