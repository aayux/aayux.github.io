I enjoy solving problems in theoretical computer science and algorithm design that require some degree of probabilistic analysis. Somewhat laterally, I am also interested in the impact of data-driven algorithms on social groups in the context of privacy, fairness and equitable social outcomes.

In addition to computer science and mathematics, I also study the rhetoric of university admissions with my wonderful partner, Erin Miller.

Graduate education is a big decision for anyone, perhaps more so if you are an international student. The application process can seem rather opaque and awfully perplexing. If you are an aspiring STEM graduate student, please feel free to reach out with questions.
